Insalata Caprese

Because this salad is so simple, fresh, top-quality tomatoes and mozzarella are important.

Per Serving: 311 calories; protein 17.9g; carbohydrates 6.6g; fat 23.9g; cholesterol 59.8mg; sodium 627.3mg.
